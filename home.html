Model training

Overview

The goal of a text-to-image model like Stable Diffusion is to produce “accurate” images based on a text prompt from the user.

Assume we are training a model “from scratch”. 

 

Model training the process of teaching a machine learning model. For text-to-image models, the model learns from training examples composed of images and their associated captions. Training can either be “from scratch” (the model starts with no knowledge) or “fine tuning” (the model begins with some knowledge, but you teach it additional things).
